Bryan Adams music world...!

Canadian rock singer , guitarist , composer and photographer .

Adams was born in Canada in 1959 to British parents. His father Conrad J. Adams was active in the military diplomatic service, his mother Elisabeth Jane Watson worked as a teacher and librarian. Because of the father's occupation, the family had to move frequently; for example, they lived in Israel and Austria and from 1967 to 1971 in Portugal . The parents separated in the mid-1970s. Bryan Adams moved to Ottawa and a little later to Vancouver with his mother and two years younger brother . In addition to English, he also speaks French .
